San Francisco Scavenger hunt Map

Instructions

There are 34 different things to find on the map.

Some require you to actually visit the place, others simply a photo the "thing" from a suitable distance away

Map below is interactive and you can look at the Ghost Pins which show you where things are - note some "things" can be in non specific places so their pins are generically placed and you have to keep an eye out for them on you wanders

The finish spot is Musee Mechanique (Red Ghost)

Given the time that you have you won't be able to find everything as it is spread over too large an area so plan first Pro tip you can use the local transportation network
